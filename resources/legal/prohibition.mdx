# Solayer Card Prohibited Activities List

This Prohibited Activities List outlines activities that are strictly forbidden when using the Solayer Card and related services. Violation of any of the following may result in card suspension, account termination, and/or legal action.

## 1. Illegal Activity

Solayer Cards may not be used for any illegal purposes, including but not limited to:

- Purchasing or selling illegal drugs, controlled substances, or prescription medications without a valid license
- Trading firearms, ammunition, explosives, or similar weapons
- Purchasing forged documents, hacking tools, or unauthorized software
- Engaging in money laundering, terrorist financing, tax evasion, or fraudulent behavior

## 2. Sanctioned or Regulated Use

The Solayer Card must not be used in violation of applicable sanctions or export control laws, including:

- Transactions involving individuals or entities sanctioned by the U.S. Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC)
- Transactions involving embargoed or restricted countries
- High-risk industries such as defense or political funding without prior Solayer approval

## 3. Cash-Equivalent Transactions

Solayer reserves the right to prohibit or restrict transactions that are considered cash-equivalent, including:

- Purchasing cryptocurrency, foreign currency, gold, or other precious metals
- Gambling transactions, including lottery tickets, casino chips, or betting platforms
- Peer-to-peer transfers or loan services not authorized by Solayer

## 5. Platform Abuse or Security Threats

Solayer prohibits any attempt to abuse or compromise the platform, including:

- Using bots, scripts, or automation to manipulate transactions
- Attempting to hack, reverse-engineer, or bypass Solayer systems
- Interfering with API, dashboard, or payment flow integrity

## 6. Prohibited Commercial Use

The Solayer Card is designed for personal consumer use. The following commercial activities are not allowed:

- Renting, sharing, or reselling your Solayer Card to third parties
- Engaging in organized schemes to exploit card rewards or benefits
- Using the Solayer brand or assets for marketing without permission

## 7. Other Activities Deemed Harmful by Solayer

Solayer may prohibit additional activities at its discretion, including:

- Violations of the Solayer User Terms or other applicable agreements
- Activities that conflict with card network or regulatory policies
- Any actions that Solayer reasonably considers harmful to its brand, users, or compliance efforts
