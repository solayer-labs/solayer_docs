# How to Stake SOL via Native Staking on Solayer

Native Staking on Solayer allows users to stake their SOL directly with the Solayer validator—offering a simple, secure way to earn staking rewards without receiving liquid tokens like sSOL.

This guide walks you through how to natively stake your SOL using Solayer’s UI.

---

## step 1: Open Native Staking Panel

Go to [https://app.solayer.org](https://app.solayer.org) and connect your wallet.  
From the **sSOL** section, click on the **Native Staking** link in the top right corner of the staking interface.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://github.com/SolayerDev/solayer_docs/blob/V2/tutorials/image/native/staking/step1.png?raw=true" alt="" />
</Frame>

---

## step 2: Enter Amount to Stake

Enter the amount of SOL you want to natively stake.  
You’ll see your current balance and USD equivalent. Once ready, click **Deposit**.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://github.com/SolayerDev/solayer_docs/blob/V2/tutorials/image/native/staking/step2.png?raw=true" alt="" />
</Frame>

---

## step 3: Approve the Transaction

Your wallet will request approval for the transaction.  
It will show a **staking account transfer** message and the estimated network fee. Click **Approve** to proceed.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://github.com/SolayerDev/solayer_docs/blob/V2/tutorials/image/native/staking/step3.png?raw=true" alt="" />
</Frame>

---

## step 4: Transaction Processing

Once approved, a transaction confirmation will appear in the top right corner of the screen.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://github.com/SolayerDev/solayer_docs/blob/V2/tutorials/image/native/staking/step4.png?raw=true" alt="" />
</Frame>

---

## step 5: Staked Balance Updates

You can now see your staked balance reflected in the Native Staking panel.  
To increase your position, repeat the process with additional SOL.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://github.com/SolayerDev/solayer_docs/blob/V2/tutorials/image/native/staking/step5.png?raw=true" alt="" />
</Frame>

---

## Summary

Native Staking with Solayer provides a direct and trusted way to delegate your SOL to one of the network’s highest-performing validators.  
It’s ideal for users who prefer not to use liquid staking tokens and still want to earn reliable staking rewards.
