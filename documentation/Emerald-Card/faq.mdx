## Card Application & Issuance

**Q1. What is the Solayer Emerald Card?**  
The Solayer Emerald Card is a Visa-based crypto debit card for eligible Solayer users to spend their crypto. Community Sale participants and Airdrop Recipients are automatically whitelisted and eligible to apply.

**Q2. Who is eligible for the Solayer Emerald Card?**  
Community Sale and Genesis Drop participants are included in the whitelist and can claim the Solayer Emerald Card from the Card page of the Solayer app.  
General users will be able to purchase a card later.

**Q4. What is the application process for the card?**  
- **Claim Your Card** – Apply on the Card page of the Solayer app  
- **Eligibility** – Community Sale and Genesis Drop participants only  
- **Requirements** –  
  - Must be 18+ with KYC verification  
  - Must reside in a supported region  
  - Community Sale users may skip KYC by sharing data via Buidlpad  
- **Activation Fee** – $10 for Genesis Drop users, free for Community Sale users  
- **Application** – Complete the card application form  
- **KYC Verification & Approval** – 3–5 business days  
- **Virtual Card** – Use immediately upon approval via Apple Pay or Google Pay  

**Q5. Will a physical card be available?**  
Yes, a physical card will be available soon.

## Card Features & Usage

**Q6. Where can I use the Solayer Emerald Card?**  
Anywhere Visa is accepted — online, offline, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

**Q7. What is the difference between a virtual card and a physical card?**  
- **Virtual Card** – Instantly issued for online purchases and mobile wallets  
- **Physical Card** – Enables ATM withdrawals and in-store use (coming soon)

**Q9. How can I add a Solayer virtual card to my mobile wallet?**  
Please refer to the wallet adding tutorial.

**Q10. Can I withdraw cash from an ATM?**  
Yes, with the physical card (coming soon). Local ATM fees may apply.

<br/>

## Fees & Transactions

**Q11. What fees apply to the Solayer Emerald Card?**  
- **Top-Up Fee** – 1% per top-up  
- **Transaction Fees** –  
  - USD Transactions: $0.15  
  - International Transactions: $0.10 + 1.5%

**Q12. Can I use the card for international payments?**  
Yes, with Visa’s real-time exchange rate. Fees apply: $0.10 + 1.5%.

<br/>

## Community Sale Participants

**Q13. What are the additional benefits for Community Sale participants?**  
Free activation and waived application fee.

**Q14. What happens if I do not apply for the card?**  
Whitelisted users must apply within the designated period.

**Q15. Do Community Sale participants need to complete KYC again?**  
Yes, all users must reconfirm their KYC. Additional documentation may be required.
