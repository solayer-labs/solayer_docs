# Venmo

Step-by-step guide to linking your Solayer Emerald Card to Venmo for seamless digital payments in the United States.

Venmo is a widely-used U.S. mobile payment app. You can add your **Solayer Emerald Card (Visa)** to Venmo for both **peer-to-peer (P2P) transfers** and **merchant transactions**.

---

## Step 1: Open the Venmo App

- Launch the Venmo app on your smartphone.  
- Log in to your Venmo account.

---

## Step 2: Navigate to Wallet

- Tap the **“Me”** tab (bottom right profile icon).  
- Tap **“Wallet.”**

---

## Step 3: Add Your Solayer Emerald Card

- In the **“Banks and Cards”** section, swipe left and tap **“Add a bank or card…”**  
- Select **“Card.”**  
- Open the **Solayer app → Card tab → View details.**

---

## Step 4: Enter Card Information

Copy and paste the following:

- Card Number  
- Expiration Date (MM/YY)  
- CVV (3-digit security code)

---

## Step 5: Confirm

- Tap **“Add.”**  
- Venmo will verify your card.  
- Upon success, it will appear under your **Payment Methods**.

---

## Important Usage Notes

Compatible with:  
- Venmo **peer-to-peer (P2P) transfers**  
- **Online checkouts** via authorized merchants  
- **QR code payments** in stores that accept Visa cards through Venmo  

**U.S. usage only** – international use is generally **not supported.**  

**Identity verification** may be required for **high-value transactions.**  

You can add up to **4 cards within a 6-month period**. Even removed cards count toward this limit.  

A **3% fee** may apply when using credit cards for payments. Some card issuers may classify Venmo payments as **cash advances**, which may incur additional fees.
