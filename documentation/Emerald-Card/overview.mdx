# Solayer Emerald Card Overview

The **Solayer Emerald Card** allows users to spend their on-chain crypto assets anywhere Visa is accepted — with **instant transactions**, enabling seamless global payments.

<br/>

## Key Features & Benefits

- **Global Usability** – Use anywhere Visa is accepted (100M+ merchants worldwide).
- **Crypto-to-Fiat Payments** – Instantly spend **USDC on Solana** without prior conversion.
- **Ecosystem Engagement** – Participate in and strengthen the **Solayer Ecosystem**.
- **Real-World Card** – Use for **online**, **in-store**, and **mobile payments** (Apple Pay, Google Pay).

<br/>

## How to Apply?

### Claim Your Card
Apply through the **Solayer App**.

### Check Eligibility

You must:
- Be **18+** with **KYC verification**.
- Live in a **supported region**.
- Be a **Community Sale participant** or **airdrop recipient** (currently required for application).

### Card Activation Fee

- **$10 activation fee**
- **Free** for **Community Sale participants**

### Application Process

1. **Submit** the card application form in the app.
2. **KYC Verification & Approval** – Takes **3–5 business days**.
3. **Instant Virtual Card** – Use immediately with **Apple Pay** or **Google Pay**.

### Top-Up with Crypto

Deposit **USDC** and bridge the gap between the crypto world and real-world spending.

<br/>

## Ecosystem Engagement

Using the Solayer Card helps **expand the Solayer Ecosystem**.

- Enjoy real-world utility while engaging with the ecosystem.
- A **dedicated Solayer Ecosystem pool** is available to reward ecosystem contributions.
